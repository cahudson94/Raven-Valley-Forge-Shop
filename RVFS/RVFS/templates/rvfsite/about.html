THE FORGE

Ravenmoore Valley Forge & Metalworks is crewed by a team of designers and artisans who will help you bring your creations to life. We offer a variety of metalworking services out of our forge and fabrication shops, including made-to-order custom fabrication, maintenance and repair of metalwork items, removal of scrap metal and materials in addition to a catalog of pre-made metal tools and artwork. Our capabilities as a shop are constantly advancing as we add new tools to our shops and new skills to our repertoire to refine our craft and our wares.

THE MARKETPLACE

RVFM also stands for the other half of who we are; Ravenmoore Valley Forge & Marketplace. Many types of products can be found here made by the members of the shop and fellow artisans alike. Small hobby artists searching for a table or a market to sell their creations or even other artists to collaborate with join us in our ventures.
